Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 10, 2026

Notifli ("we," "us," or "our") operates the notifli.app website and provides AI-powered SMS appointment reminder services for small businesses. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our service.

By using our service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Business Information

When you register for an account, we collect:

  • Business name
  • Business email address
  • Industry type
  • Timezone
  • Account credentials (encrypted password)

1.2 Client Data

You may upload your clients' information to send appointment reminders:

  • Client names
  • Client phone numbers
  • Appointment dates and times
  • Service descriptions

Important: You are responsible for obtaining consent from your clients to receive SMS messages. Notifli acts as a data processor; you remain the data controller.

1.3 Usage Information

We automatically collect:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Device information
  • Usage patterns and analytics

1.4 Payment Information

Payment processing is handled by Stripe, Inc. We do not store your credit card information. Stripe collects and processes payment data in accordance with their privacy policy.

1.5 Communication Data

We store:

  • SMS message content sent through our platform
  • Delivery status and timestamps
  • Client responses (CONFIRM/CANCEL replies)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data for:

  • Service Delivery: Sending automated SMS appointment reminders
  • Account Management: User authentication and account access
  • Billing: Processing subscription payments
  • Communication: Sending service updates, security alerts, and support messages
  • Analytics: Understanding usage patterns to improve our service
  • Compliance: Meeting legal and regulatory requirements
  • Security: Detecting and preventing fraud and unauthorized access

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share data only in the following circumstances:

3.1 Service Providers

We work with trusted third-party providers:

  • Twilio Inc. - SMS message delivery
  • Stripe, Inc. - Payment processing
  • Railway.app - Cloud hosting infrastructure

These providers have access only to the data necessary to perform their functions and are prohibited from using it for other purposes.

3.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose data if required by law, subpoena, or legal process, or to protect our rights, property, or safety, or the rights, property, or safety of others.

3.3 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, customer information may be transferred as part of the transaction. Users will be notified via email before data becomes subject to a different privacy policy.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using SSL/TLS
  • Password Security: Passwords are hashed using bcrypt with salt
  • Access Controls: Role-based access to internal systems
  • Database Security: Encrypted at rest with regular security audits
  • Regular Updates: Security patches applied promptly

However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your data, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for the following periods:

  • Active Accounts: Data is retained while your account is active
  • Cancelled Accounts: Data is deleted within 30 days of account cancellation
  • Message Logs: SMS delivery logs retained for 90 days for troubleshooting
  • Payment Records: Financial records retained for 7 years as required by tax law

You may request data deletion at any time by contacting support@notifli.app.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability: Request transfer of your data to another service

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@notifli.app.

By using Notifli, you acknowledge and agree:

  • You are responsible for obtaining consent from your clients to receive SMS messages

Notifli provides the platform for SMS delivery but does not assume liability for your compliance with telecom regulations.

You can disable cookies through your browser settings, but this may limit service functionality.

Our service is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are under 18, do not use our service or provide us with any personal information.

By using our service, you consent to this transfer.
