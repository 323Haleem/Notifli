FAQ
Everything you need to know
Do my clients need to download an app? +

No. Clients receive a regular SMS text message to their phone. Nothing to download, no account needed. It works on every phone.

How does the AI personalization work? +

When you add an appointment, Notifli's AI generates a unique reminder message based on your business type, the client's name, the specific service, and the appointment time. Each message feels personal and hand-written — not like a generic robot blast.

What happens when a client replies CANCEL? +

The appointment status updates automatically in your dashboard to "Cancelled." You'll see it immediately, giving you time to fill that slot with another client before the appointment time passes.

Does it work with my existing booking software? +

Notifli works as a standalone reminder system. You add appointments directly into Notifli. Native integrations with popular booking systems (Square, Acuity, Vagaro) are on our roadmap and coming soon.

Is there a contract or long-term commitment? +

Zero contracts. Month-to-month. Cancel anytime with no fees or penalties. We're confident you'll see results — if not, there's nothing holding you.

How much does the SMS messaging cost? +

SMS messaging costs are included in your plan — no per-message fees or surprise bills. Your $49/mo covers everything for unlimited appointments on the Pro plan.

Is my client data secure? +

Yes. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We never sell or share your client data. You own your data and can export it at any time.
